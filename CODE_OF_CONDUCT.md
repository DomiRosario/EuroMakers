# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback.
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences.
- Taking responsibility, apologizing, and learning from mistakes.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional community setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code,
wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this
Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and also applies when an
individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

To report violations, contact: contact@euromakers.org

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
